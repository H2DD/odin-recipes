Stuffed Peppers

Description:

A classic recipe for Stuffed Bell Peppers stuffed filled with meat, rice, and sauteed vegetables then baked in a delicious sauce. This classic recipe is so easy to make and comfort food at its finest.

Ingredients:

  • Bell Peppers(yellow, red and green)
  • White or Brown Rice
  • Shredded Sharp Sheddar
  • Onions
  • 5 chicken breats
  • Tomatoes
  • A single can of Tomato Sauce
  • Salt
  • Black Beans
  • Black Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Meat thermometer
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Jalapenos
  • butter
  • Cilantro
  • lime
  • Coconut milk
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375
  2. Cut the tops of Bell peppers and clean out any seeds
  3. Cut the core out of the the bell peppers
  4. Once the oven is warm, put the bell peppers in on a sheet pan for 10 minutes to soften them before stuffing
  5. take them out after 10 minutes
  6. Keep the oven on but turn it up to 400 degrees
  7. Measure 1 cup of rice, pour it in the pot and rinse thoroughly until the water is clear
  8. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil.
  9. Once the water begins to boil place a lid over the pot and turn the heat to low. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes
  10. Rinse chicen with cold water and scrub them with lime
  11. Season chicken and place in the over to cook for about 30 minutes. Regularly check the chicken temperature for 165 degrees
  12. While waiting chop up 1 whole white onion,1 head of cilantro, 2 Jalapenos, 1 tomatoe and 4 garlic cloves
  13. Pour some oil in a skillet and wait until it heats.
  14. As it heats put the onions, cilantro, jalapenos, tomatoe and garlic cloves
  15. Let them carmalize in the pot
  16. Once they give off a brownish color pour in 1 cup of coconut milk.
  17. Pour the can of tomato sauce in.
  18. Season the sauce with Salt, Pepper and Paprika
  19. Once it begins to bubble turn the sauce on low and begin to add the shredded cheese starting with a cup and add more as seen fit
  20. Stir the cheese in as you add it.
  21. Take the chicken out,place it into a bowl and begin to shred it with two forks.
  22. Add all of the shredded chicken into the sauce and stir it as you add it.
  23. Add the rice to the sauce as well and stir it.
  24. one Everything is added you can use tongs or a ladel to put the mix into the Bell Peppers (stuff to the brim).
  25. Add cheese to the top of each Bell Peppers
  26. Place the bell peppers back in the oven for about 10 minutes
  27. At the 10 minute mark turn the oven to broil and leave them in for another 2-3 minutes.
  28. Take them out, let them cool for 10 minutes and Enjoy!
