Honey Garlic Chicken Wraps

Description

Here is how to make honey garlic chicken Wraps

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups warm cooked rice
  • 8 (8 inch) soft flour tortillas (Can also substitue with lettuce)
  • ½ cup carrot matchsticks, or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix honey, soy sauce, cornstarch, and garlic together in a small bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir chicken until no longer pink, 5 to 8 minutes. Pour honey mixture over chicken; cook and stir until sauce thickens and coats chicken, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Divide warm rice among tortillas; spoon chicken on top. Cover chicken with carrots. Roll up wraps.
  4. Enjoy!
